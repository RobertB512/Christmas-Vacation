Welcome to Winter Wonder!

Here at Winter Wonder, we aim to make your Christmas or winter vacation one you won't soon forget! We offer events and activities for young and old, so everyone can find something to enjoy and bring back with them to wherever they live!

We offer rooms for

Individuals

Families of sizes 3 to 12

Couples

You'll be able to do all this and more

Listen to or join in on some caroling

We have caroling every evening from 5 to 7. Come and join us. If you can't, we might come to you and sing a song.

Take a sleighride

Come on our magical peaceful sleighride. The views are gorgeous and one you'll want to ride again before you leave.

Treelighting ceromoney

Our tree lightning ceromoney takes place on the 23rd of December at 7pm. We take great care in decorating it with lights and ornaments. It takes place in the center of or property. You can even get a great view from some of our rooms, if you don't want to go out in the cold.

Snowbording and Skiing

You'll be able to snowbard and ski. Although we don't offer any of those tall ramps, we do have different areas for different skill levels. Watch out for the signs though, the'll tell you when you're going too far.

See Santa

On the 15th of every December the jolly man himself comes to visit.
